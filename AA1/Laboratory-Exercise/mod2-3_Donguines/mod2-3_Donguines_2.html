My Favorite OPM Band

This is SUD. A current line-up of 5 members strong with Sud on vocals and guitar, Marc Reyes on bass, Jimbo Cuenco on drums, Kohl Aguilar on keys, Carlos Dela Fuente on sax, the band is still on a mission for your pleasure, but way better and way more different this time around. They make great music especially songs about falling in love with someone.
